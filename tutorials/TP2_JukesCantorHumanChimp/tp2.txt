$$

q(r,T) = (1 - e^{-r_{JC}2T})*3/4
q(r,T) = (1 - e^{-r*4/3*2T})*3/4
q(r,T) = (1 - e^{-r*8/3*T})*3/4
$$

$$
k = N*q(r,T)
$$

$$
k_{obs} = 11 =  N*q(r,T) = N*(1 - e^{-r*8/3*T})*3/4
k_{obs} /N =  (1-e^{-r*8/3*T})*3/4
1-4/3 k_{obs} /N = e^{-r*8/3*T}
log(1-4/3 k_{obs} /N) = -r*8/3*T
T = \frac{log(1-4/3 k_{obs} /N)}{-r*8/3}
$$

Numerical application:
$$
T = log(1-11/878*4/3)/(-0.00066*8/3)
T = 9.57
$$
